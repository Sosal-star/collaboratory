Popular Trips!

Pokhara

Pokhara, nestled in the majestic Himalayas of Nepal, is a picturesque city renowned for its stunning natural beauty and serene ambiance. Situated around the tranquil Phewa Lake, Pokhara offers breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks, including the iconic Annapurna range and the towering Machhapuchhre (Fishtail) peak.

Kathmandu

Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, is a vibrant and culturally rich metropolis nestled in the Kathmandu Valley. With a history spanning over 2,000 years, Kathmandu is a melting pot of ancient traditions, religious landmarks, and bustling urban life.

Mustang

Mustang is a remote and mystical region located in the northern part of Nepal, bordering Tibet. Known as the "Forbidden Kingdom," Mustang remained isolated from the outside world for centuries, preserving its ancient culture, traditions, and breathtaking landscapes.
